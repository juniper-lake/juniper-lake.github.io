About

My name is Juniper Lake, but I was previously known as Danielle Swanner and Danielle Novick just cause unnecessary confusion. I am a PhD candidate at the University of Delaware Center for Bioinformatics and Computational Biology, expecting to graduate in Fall 2020. More importantly, I am an avid mountain biker, swing dancer, rock climber, banjo player, dog owner, and cook, among other things. In both my work and my personal life I enjoy solving problems (e.g. troubleshooting code, repairing cars and home appliances) and learning new skills.
